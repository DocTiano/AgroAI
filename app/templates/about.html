About AgroAI

AgroAI

Empowering farmers with AI technology

Our Mission

AgroAI aims to revolutionize crop disease management by providing farmers with accessible, accurate, and timely disease detection tools. By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and computer vision, we help farmers identify plant diseases early, reduce crop losses, and promote sustainable farming practices.

The Technology

Our system uses advanced Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) trained on thousands of plant leaf images to accurately identify various crop diseases. The AI model can detect subtle patterns and symptoms that might be missed by the human eye, providing farmers with reliable diagnostic information.

How It Works

Capture

Take a clear photo of the affected plant leaf

Upload

Upload the image to our AI-powered platform

Diagnose

Receive instant disease identification with treatment recommendations

Our Team

AgroAI was developed by a team of students from Arellano University's School of Information Technology Education (SITe) as part of their research project. The team combined expertise in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and agricultural science to create a solution that addresses real-world farming challenges.
